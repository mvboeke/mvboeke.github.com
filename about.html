Designer\Product guy\Startup veteran

I like to focus on applying design thinking and modern technology to shake-up traditional (read:boring) industries. I currently design and manage products at Braintree.

Prior to Braintree, I launched the first web-based CRM and accounting platforms for hedge funds at Backstop. Previous to that, I designed the CarePages social support platform for hospital patients, which is now part of the Everyday Health network.

I live, work, and run countless miles in the wonderful city of Chicago. I like to write about product design and startup life at on this site and on Twitter at @mvboeke.
